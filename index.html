🎱 Pool Aim Assistant

This app uses your camera to overlay an aiming line on the pool table.

1. Tap the white cue ball
2. Tap the ball you want to pot
3. Tap the pocket

The app will then show you exactly where to aim and the "ghost ball" contact point.
